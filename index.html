About Us

A Travel Agency That
Actually Listens to You

We started Royal Wings Travel right here in Jamshedpur because we saw too many people getting overcharged or stuck with generic tour packages that didn't fit their needs.

Our approach is simple — sit down, understand what you're looking for (budget, interests, travel dates), and then build something that works specifically for you.
