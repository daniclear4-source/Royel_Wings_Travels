Our Journey

The Royal Wings Story

Founded in 1984 near the historic Sonari Airport in Jamshedpur, Royal Wings Travel began with a single vision: to transform how the steel city explores the world. What started as a dedicated ticketing counter has blossomed into a full-service, premium travel consultant managing itineraries across 7 continents.

Over the years, we have forged strong global alliances with major luxury hotel chains, trusted local car rentals, and international airlines. These relationships ensure our clients receive royal status everywhere they travel, combined with unmatched pricing benefits.

We pride ourselves on our core team of passionate trip planners who inspect resorts, sail on cruises, and experience tours first-hand before suggesting them to our clients. When you book with us, you are booking a verified, flawless experience.
